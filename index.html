Event Overview ★

SMCC-CCIS Week 2026 is an annual campus event that highlights student projects, programming skills, innovation, cybersecurity awareness, and collaboration among future IT professionals.

Join us for a week of learning, networking, and fun & excitement as we celebrate technology in education. ✓
